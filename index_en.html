Services

Guest House Saint Marina is located about 100m from the north beach. Nearby are also the city center and the south beach. We offer coziness, a peaceful stay and a beautiful garden with dining tables.

Each room contains air conditioning, hairdryer, refrigerator and freezer.

Free internet and TV in every room.

Terrace and a safe in every room.

Free Wi-Fi in every room but also in the house's garden.

A beautiful garden with flowers.

Dining tables in the garden.
